The Gap

Northern Ireland recorded 13 first-equity deals in 2023. Across all sectors. For a region with genuine technical depth — a strong university pipeline, world-class cyber and fintech clusters, and a generation of founders who've watched Belfast become a serious tech hub — the funding infrastructure still isn't built for what comes next.

Most early-stage capital here is milestone-based, syndicate-led, or structured around de-risking for a seed round that may never arrive. Nobody is writing a first cheque into an AI-native company at the idea stage. Not because the talent isn't here. Because the fund doesn't exist.

Blanche is that fund.

What We Back

We back founders with deep domain knowledge who are using AI to rebuild something from first principles. Not wrappers. Not productivity tools. Genuine reconstruction — where the incumbent model becomes structurally untenable.

The kind of founder we're looking for

A former insurance underwriter rebuilding how commercial risk is priced and assessed.

A logistics operator who knows exactly where the inefficiencies are — and why they've survived this long.

A healthcare administrator who understands why clinical pathways fail and what a system designed for AI looks like instead.

A compliance professional who sees that entire functions will be automated — and is building the replacement.

The pattern is someone who has spent years inside a system that's about to break, and is building what replaces it.
